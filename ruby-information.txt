RVM stands for Ruby Version Manager.

What is Ruby?

Ruby is a dynamic, reflective, object-oriented, general-purpose programming language.
It was designed and developed by Yukihiro Matsumoto in the mid-1990's.

What are the benefits of using Ruby?

Ruby has an elegant syntax and is extremely easy to read and write. 
It's easy to learn for beginners and has enough depth for experts to explore.

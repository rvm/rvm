Using rvm rubies with Passenger

rvm will allow you to use any of it's MRI/YARV rubies with passenger very easily.

Example

Let us say that we have used a custom rvm installed ruby-1.8.6-p369. To use this ruby under Passenger with Nginx simply set:

passenger_ruby /home/wayne/.rvm/bin/passenger-ruby-1.8.6-p369;

And for Passenger running under Apache set:

PassengerRuby /home/wayne/.rvm/bin/passenger-ruby-1.8.6-p369;

Where you substitute '/home/wayne/' with your own ~/ and 'ruby-1.8.6-369' with your particular interpreter-version-patchlevel string.
